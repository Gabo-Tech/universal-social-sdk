# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Focus on ideas, code quality, and outcomes.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Accept feedback gracefully and provide it clearly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing, intimidation, or sustained disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to project maintainers.

- Preferred contact: open a private security report or maintainer contact channel.
- Include context, links, and timestamps where possible.

All reports are reviewed promptly and handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take actions they deem appropriate, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from community spaces.

## Attribution

This policy is inspired by common open source community standards, including the Contributor Covenant.
